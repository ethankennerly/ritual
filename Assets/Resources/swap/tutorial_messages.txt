In this ritual, you swap two letters to spell a word. Click "W," then drag to "I" to spell "WISH."
You can spell any connected letters. Click "R," then drag to to "I" to spell "RITE."
You can spell two words with a swap.  Click "M" and drag to "S", to spell "MAGIC" and "SPELL".  
The letter to swap will always be the first letter of the word.  Find all the words to reach the next level.
